Beas Valley

Beas Valley, nestled in the scenic Himalayan region of Himachal Pradesh, India, is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes and the meandering Beas River. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks, this picturesque valley attracts nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike, offering a perfect blend of tranquility and outdoor excitement.

Kheerganga Rudranath

Kheerganga, situated in the Parvati Valley of Himachal Pradesh, is famed for its natural hot springs and stunning mountain vistas, making it a popular trekking destination. Rudranath, on the other hand, is a sacred Hindu pilgrimage site in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand, dedicated to Lord Shiva, where the Rudranath Temple stands amidst pristine Himalayan beauty.
